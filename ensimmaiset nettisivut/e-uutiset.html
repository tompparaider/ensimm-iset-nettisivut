The world's best footballer selected!

20/04/2015

Great news! FC Peräkylän Potku's Captain Robert Landrooster was selected as the 2015's world best football player, a whopping nine months before the end of the year!

Robert was awarded the golden football. In the picture you can see Robert celebrating his victory.

For behalf of the whole FC Peräkylän Potku's organization, Congratulations Robert!





Streaker at Peräkylän Potku's practice match!

07/04/2015

In FC Peräkylän Potku's practice match against HJK we saw things on the pitch that do not belong there.

One of HJK's supporters invaded the pitch wearing only his underwear. The streaker has not yet been identified, but the police are said to be investigating the incident.

HJK has apologized for the incident.





Peräkylän Potku's attacker got almost injured!

1/2/2015

FC Peräkylän Potku's attacker Niklas Lahtikannas was horribly kicked in the face while representing the Spanish national team. Mexico's Juan Taco tried to win the ball by reaching with his foot, while Lahtikannas tried to header the ball.

Thanks to his thick skull, Lahtikannas survived without any injuries. He is fit for play in Peräkylän Potku's next game against the Espoo's Eunukit.
